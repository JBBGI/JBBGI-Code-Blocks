Why Students Love It

The Omano OM-118-M4 monocular microscope is the sister microscope to the OM118-M3 but with an additional 100x objective lens and a mechanical slide holder for easier slide handling. Recommended for K-12, students love this microscope because of its durable build, and its unique combination of features that make it an inspiring perfect learning tool.

 

Packed with Optimal Optics for Students

The Omano OM118-M4 is a high-power monocular microscope used for viewing cellular structures on slides. This student compound microscope features:

  • An ocular angled at an ergonomic 45 degrees that rotates through 360 degrees for flexible viewing. 
  • A single WF10x eyepiece that combines four achromatic objectives lenses (4x, 10x, 40x spring, 100x spring oil immersion).
  • Magnification levels of 40x, 100x, 400x and 1,000x.

 

 

Goodbye, Weak Illumination!

The OM118-M4 is built with a professional Abbe 1.25 N.A condenser to train students how to use professional-grade tools, should they decide to pursue further education in the sciences.

It also includes transmitted 20w incandescent illumination that allows for variable intensity.

 

Focus on the Stage

The OM118-M4 includes a plain stage with a mechanical slide holder that enables easy slide movements on the stage, perfect for inexperienced or beginner students.

 

The focusing mechanism on the OM118-M4 is unique, and built to keep students focused and engaged. The mechanism features a rack and pinion design for durability and coaxial coarse and fine focus controls. We recommend coaxial coarse/fine focus controls since they enable 'eyes down' focusing. In other words, the viewer does not constantly need to look up in order to find the other focus control.

 

Additional safety features include:

 

  • A rack stop to ensure that the stage does not interfere with the objectives and cause damage to the slide.
  • A slip clutch control to mitigate the risk of damaging the focus controls through overextension.

 

Swap Out that Hard Eyepiece for Soft Imaging

Technology is a part of our day-to-day lives. Weaning students off technology and putting them onto a microscope is a challenge for many educators.

 

What if there was a way to combine a love of technology with a passion for microscopy?

 

You can pair the OM118-M4 with a digital microscope camera to make this compound microscope into a digital microscope in an instant. The Summit Series cameras are known for their remarkable color resolution and color sensitivity, meaning your students can study bright, clear, and vivid images under their scope.

 

In addition, the 0.5x reducing lens makes for an image that is significantly closer to that seen through the eyepieces with the naked eye. A microscope camera comes with robust documentation and measuring software that your students will love using to play around with their images.

 

 

Other features of microscope cameras include:

 

  • Fast refresh rates 
  • Auto/manual exposure
  • White balance control
  • Fullscreen live imaging 
  • Time-lapse photography 
  • User-friendly image capture
