Why Students Will Love It

The OM118-B4L is built for students learning basic life science processes in grade 4 to senior high school students preparing for post-secondary education in the sciences.

It features all the flexible design features students need to thrive, like:

 

  • An integrated double-layered mechanical stage that includes full graduated X/Y movement (18mm x 70mm) with coaxial coarse/fine focus controls.
  • High-quality rack and pinion mechanics for longer life and coaxial focus controls for easy focusing.
  • An Abbe 1.25 N. A condenser with an Iris diaphragm for optimal adjustments.
  • A binocular microscope head inclined at 45 degreesto make constant viewing more ergonomic and posture-friendly.
  • A head that rotates through 360 degrees for flexible viewing positions and easy collaboration.
  • Bright built-in LED illumination that offers variable intensity.
  • A rack stop to prevent slide breakage.
  • A slip clutch control to reduce the risk of damage to focus controls caused by over-extension.

Why You’ll Love It

The OM2300S Zoom Stereo Microscope microscope is the holy grail for our industrial inspection customers. It features all the solid design features inspectors need, like:

  • Crisp and clean optics. The 7X-45X zoom range (6.4:1) is parfocal with dual diopters integrated into the oculars. The interpupillary adjustment range is 55mm-75mm.
  • A sturdy single-arm boom stand (with an included 84mm focus block). The pole is 17" tall with an effective usable height of 12". The horizontal arm extension is 20" including the microscope head.
  • A 4.5" working distance (which increases to 6.5" with the addition of 0.5x auxiliary Barlow lens).
  • WF10X eyepieces and eye guards, , available in both binocular or trinocular format.
  • Dual hand control. Rack and pinion focus includes controls for both your right and left hands to make focusing a lot more convenient.

 

Say Goodbye to Weak Illumination

Besides its reliable workhorse build, the OM2300S-JW11 is versatile. Before you put it in your cart, you can customize:

  • The head type (binocular or trinocular)
  • The illumination type
  • The camera type

… to suit your application.

 

Swap Out that Hard Eyepiece for Soft Imaging

Adding a digital microscope camera to your OM2300S-JW11 microscope reduces that neck pain and eye strain by showing you your images on a computer screen instead.
