What’s So Special About the Stand?

 

The V10 stand features a useful 4", adjustable extension bar at the tip of the boom arm. When you’re using this extension with your eyes down, you can easily adjust the horizontal extension of the boom.

 

In other words, there is no need to release the entire bar in order to make small adjustments. The V10 boom stand is also, heavy-duty weighing 48lbs. The pole is 20" tall with 14.5" usable height. The horizontal arm extends 26" including the microscope head.

 

Let’s Get Optical

The OM99-V10 is built with sharp optics that compete with those of big brand names (without the big brand name prices, of course). 

 

  • Dual diopters are integrated into the eye tubes for individual eye adjustment
  • The interpupillary adjustment range is 55mm-75mm.
  • Working distance is 31/4" and more than doubles to over 6" with the addition of an 0.5x auxiliary Barlow lens
  • Optional Barlow lenses include: 0.3x, 0.4x, 0.5x, 0.75x, 1.0x, and 2.0x

 

Say Goodbye to Weak Focus and Illumination

 

It’s time to focus on the focus. The OM99-V10’s rack and pinion focus include dual right and left-hand controls for extra convenience. 

 

This stereo microscope also comes with an 80 LED ring light with variable intensity for shadow-free illumination. If you need to take it up a notch, you can upgrade to a stronger LED when you add the microscope to your shopping cart.

 

 

Swap Out that Hard Eyepiece for Soft Imaging

Though this stereo microscope comes with rubber eyecups, nothing expands your stereo microscopy like adding a digital microscope camera does. 

The OM99-V10 stereo microscope is available in both binocular or trinocular formats, with the trinocular option including a 23mm smooth camera port. Both versions are optionally available with click-stop detents for precise zoom observations.

 

When the photo-prism lever is engaged in the OM99-Trinocular, 100% of the light from the left optical path is directed to the camera port. The image can still be viewed through the right eyepiece.
