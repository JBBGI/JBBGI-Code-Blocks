How to use a spotting scope like a pro!

Maybe you’re in the market for a new spotting scope. Or maybe you just dug one up out of the back of your closet. 

Now what?

With these simple tips, you’ll be using your spotting scope like a professional in no time. Once you’ve unboxed your new scope (or dusted off your old one), read this:


Location: Whether you’re watching birds, whales or deer, take the time to scope out the area and set up in the right spot. Don’t rush this step!

Call in support: Powerful magnification is great! But it means that the smallest shake of your hand can blur your view. Bring along the right tripod (with adjustable legs and a pan head for smooth scanning). 

Prep for the elements: With the right scope, the elements don’t have to stand in your way. Opt for a water-proof, fog-proof model to take the worry out of your adventure. Just make sure you dress the part, too!

Take breaks: As exciting as your subject may be, it’s important to rest your eyes regularly.
