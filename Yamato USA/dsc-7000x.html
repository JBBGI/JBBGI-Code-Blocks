Highest sensitivity, superior baseline stability and repeatability

In this newly designed sensor, multiple thermocouples guarantee high sensitivity, while the centric heat-flow method provides uniform and stable heat flow to sample and reference and therefore creates extremely stable baselines.

The new furnace, which is composed of a 3 layer insulation wall, suppresses the influence of baseline fluctuation, and increases temperature following capability.

Improved Cooling Performance

Newly designed cooling systems including Auto LN 2 Gas Cooling System and Electronic Cooling System are available for the DSC7000 Series. The cooling performances of both systems are improved.
